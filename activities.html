Chinese tea culture is extensive and profound, and tea was discovered by Shennong 4,600 years ago. Tea is unique to China; tea is an art. Tea tasting is the sentiment of life. To like tea is the interest of life. Here you will have the opportunity to taste tea with your family and learn about traditional Kung Fu tea brewing methods.
